Values or Racism? What really turned the South Red?

Prior to 1964, the South historically voted Democrat. Fast forward to present day, the Democratic party is now the "Liberal" party, while the Republican party is the "Conservative" Party. Richard Nixon purportedly utilized the “Southern Strategy” as a way to court Southern White Democrats who were unhappy with De-Segregation. This has led many to view the Republican party, particularly in the South, as racist. Using party platform term frequency, let's discover if the South switched party lines because of Civil Rights and De-Segregation or voters re-aligned themselves based on their values.
